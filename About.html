About

Compare4Sports is a company set up by Matthew Butcher currently a student at Coventry University. The company is currently setting up a website which at the moment is currently under devlopment and is in process to be completed by March 2015. Compare4Sports or C4S is a company designed to promote different sports from around to world and to give people the knowledge that sports are played around the world in a global context and not just in your more westernised countries.
