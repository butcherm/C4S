Football

Football is the name for many sports. The most popular type of football is Association football, which is also named "soccer". Football can also mean American football (gridiron), ??(Ancient Chinese football), rugby union, rugby league, Australian Rules Football, Gaelic football or Canadian football.

The name football comes from the two words "foot" and "ball". It is named football because the players of the game walk and run (on foot) while playing, as opposed to polo and other games played on horseback. The ball is often kicked with the foot, but depending on the game, it can also be hit using the knee or head (in association football) and handling the ball is a part of many other football variants. In this section we will focus Mainly on the British rules of Football

Below are 5 random facts about Football in England

  • The Highest Ever Goalscorer in a Premier League Season was Dixie Dean for Everton in the 1927-28 season
  • Jimmy Greaves holds the all time Premier League Goalscoring record with 357 Goals
  • Liverpool hold the record for the most players to score 3 goals in a game with 36
  • There has been 271 Hat-Tricks scored since Premier League records began
  • During the 1993-94 season Swindon Town conceded 100 Goals in the season. Thats on average over 2.5 Goals a Game
